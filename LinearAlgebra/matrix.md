### Trace of a Matrix
The trace of a matrix is given by the sum of its diagonal elements.
$$\large{\color{Purple}\mathit{Tr}\mathbf{(A) =\sum_{i} A_{ii}}}$$
#### Example
Suppose we have a matrix -

$$\large{\color{Purple}\mathbf{A} = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 4 & 5 \\
7 & 2 & 6 \\ 
8 & 9 & 3 \\
\end{bmatrix} , 
Tr(\mathbf{A}) = 1+ 2+ 3= 6
} 
$$
