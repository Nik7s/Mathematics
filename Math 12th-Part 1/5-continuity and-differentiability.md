### Continuity Function
**Definition 1:**  Suppose **_f_** is a real function on a subset of the real numbers and let **_c_** be a point in the domain of **_f_**. Then **_f_** is continuous at **c** if

$$\large{\color{Purple}
\lim_{x \to c} f(x ) = f(c)
}
$$
